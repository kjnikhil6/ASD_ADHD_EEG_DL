    About

    This study aims to develop an AI system for the classification of neurological disorders using EEG signals. The disorders include Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) and autism. The system is based on deep learning techniques and utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) as the main classifier. The EEG data is preprocessed and filtered and segmented into appropriate segments before being inputted into the CNN network.

    The results of the classification process are used to determine the presence of ADHD or ASD and the associated severity. The proposed system has the potential to provide a non-invasive, reliable and efficient method for the classification of ADHD and ASD, and can be useful for medical professionals and researchers in the field
